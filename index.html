Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [05,07,25]

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the SITE-∆R Discord Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information when you interact with it on Discord. By using the Bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The Bot may collect the following types of information:

  • User Information: When you use the Bot, it may access your Discord username, user ID, and any roles you have in the server.
  • Message Data: The Bot may read and respond to messages in channels where it is present. This includes commands you issue to the Bot and any messages that are part of the command context.
  • Server Information: The Bot may collect information about the servers it is added to, including server ID, server name, and channel information.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected by the Bot is used for the following purposes:

  • Moderation: To perform moderation tasks such as kicking, banning, muting, and clearing messages as requested by authorized users.
  • Command Processing: To process commands issued by users and provide appropriate responses.
  • Access Restriction: Only users with the role @Botcommandperm are authorized to use the Bot’s commands. The Bot enforces this restriction by checking user roles before processing commands.
  • Improvement: To improve the Bot's functionality and user experience based on usage patterns and feedback.

4. Data Retention

The Bot does not store personal data permanently. User and message data are processed in real-time and are not retained beyond the duration of the command execution. However, logs may be temporarily stored for debugging purposes.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. The Bot is hosted on secure servers, and we implement reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information.

6. User Rights

As a user of the Bot, you have the right to:

  • Request information about the data we hold about you.
  • Request the deletion of your data, where applicable.
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the Bot's documentation or through a message in Discord. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support channels provided in the Bot or the Discord server where the Bot is active.
